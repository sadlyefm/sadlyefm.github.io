عيد الحب: Heart over Love

Content: Five films were selected, mirroring the five-pointed pentagram, the films show the mystical side of erotica, love, and desire. The Love Witch's Love me, Love me, Love me scene teaches us how to make a love spell. Climax embodies erotica with the captivating dance sequence. Twin Peaks's esoteric symbolism and Laura Palmer. Rocky Horror expresses queer desire and erotica. Anyab, an Egyptian cult classic and a remake of the Rocky Horror Picture Show, shows a dancing cult of a vampires.

Technique: Film footage blended using audio reaction and motion detection line tracing

Source: The Love Witch 2016 dir Anna Biller, Climax 2018 dir Gasper Noe, Twin Peaks 1990 dir David Lynch, The Rocky Horror Picture Show 1975 dir Jim Sharman, Anyab 1981 dir Mohammed Shebl, @dj_garbage
